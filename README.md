## 👨‍💻 About Me

🏢 I'm a **DevOps Engineer** at [EstateX](https://www.estatex.eu/). Where I'm building and maintaining the multi-subscription Azure infrastructure.

🔧 In my day-to-day work I'm currently focused on:
- 🏗️ **Infrastructure as Code** — Provisioning and architecting Azure infrastructure with Terraform, designing reusable modules that standardize how our teams provision resources across environments. Responsible for defining infrastructure patterns, governance guardrails, and ensuring every component follows security and compliance best practices.
- 🔁 **CI/CD Pipelines** — Building and architecting complex CI/CD pipelines with GitLab CI and GitHub Actions, including gatekeeper stages (manual approval jobs) before critical operations like `terraform apply` or `helm upgrade`. Designing pipeline templates reusable across multiple projects to enforce consistent delivery standards org-wide.
- 📊 **Observability** — Designing the observability strategy for our platform: architecting the Prometheus + Loki + Grafana stack, defining alerting standards, and building dashboards that give both engineers and stakeholders clear visibility into system health, performance, and SLOs.
- 🧭 **Solution Architecture** — Translating business and technical requirements into scalable, cloud-native architectures on Azure. Leading infrastructure design decisions, evaluating new technologies, and acting as the bridge between development teams and platform capabilities — ensuring systems are built for reliability, security, and long-term maintainability.
